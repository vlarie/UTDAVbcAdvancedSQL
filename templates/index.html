Surf's Up!

Check out Hawaii's weather history for better trip planning

Available Queries:

  • Last Year's Precipitation: /api/v1.0/precipitation
  • List of Stations: api/v1.0/stations
  • Last Year's Temperature Observations: /api/v1.0/tobs
  • Summary Stats for a Given Start Date: /api/v1.0/<start>
    • Replace <start> with your query date formatted as Year-Month-Day (e.g. 2015-08-25)
  • Summary Stats for a Given Date Range: /api/v1.0/<start>/<end>
    • Replace <start> and <end> with your query date formatted as Year-Month-Day (e.g. 2015-08-25)
